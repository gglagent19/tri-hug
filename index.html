FAQ

Does it work on twist-off caps?
It's built for pry-off crown caps (the classic kind). Twist-offs will still pop and launch, they just won't fly quite as far — physics is physics, boss.
Is it safe at a party?
The launcher itself is completely safe to handle. Flying caps, however, obey no one — never aim at faces, pets, or the DJ's setup. Recommended for ages 14+. Be the hero, not the villain.
How long does shipping take?
We dispatch within 48 hours. Delivery takes 3–6 business days across India, with tracking sent to your email. Cash on delivery available. Ordering for a weekend party? Order by Tuesday to be safe.

It's printed in tough engineering-grade material and built for thousands of pops. If it ever breaks in normal use, we replace it free — that's a promise.
